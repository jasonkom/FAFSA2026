All Major 2026–27 FAFSA Changes (November 2025 Update)

The 2026–27 FAFSA launched September 24, 2025. Key changes include full SAI replacement of EFC, elimination of sibling discount, new asset exemptions for small family businesses/farms, and foreign income now counted for Pell Grants.

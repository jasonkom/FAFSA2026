The 7 Biggest Permanent Changes for 2026–27

  1. EFC replaced by SAI (Student Aid Index)
  2. Negative SAI allowed down to –1,500
  3. 100% family farm & small-business net-worth exclusion
  4. Pell Grant maximum increased to $7,895
  5. Only ~36 questions instead of 108
  6. Every contributor must create an FSA ID
  7. Mandatory IRS Direct Data Exchange

Frequently Asked Questions

When does FAFSA 2026–27 open?

October 1, 2025 at 8 AM EST

Are family farms still counted?

No — 100% excluded if the farm/business has fewer than 100 employees.

Do siblings in college still help?

No — “number in college” is permanently removed.
